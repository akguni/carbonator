Carbonator is developed for educational purposes.

All assumptions and calculations are for illustrative purposes only.

Consumption and duration suggestions can be changed for each calculation on the main screen.

Efficiency of appliances and carbon footprint of electricity grids can vary significantly. Therefore, if something does not make sense to you, please check the underlying assumptions and try to find out the efficiency and costs applicable to your own situation.

You can put in your own values on the user settings page when you register.

Only admin users can add new appliance categories but all users can take advantage of "generic appliance" selection if none of the other labels describe the type of appliance they would like choose for calculating savings.
